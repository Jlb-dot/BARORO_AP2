Traditional Clothing & Festivals

Switzerland's cultural identity is deeply rooted in its regional traditions, costumes, and festivals. Explore the colorful world of Swiss "Tracht" (traditional clothing) and vibrant celebrations that have been preserved for generations.

Appenzell Tracht

The women's costume from Appenzell features intricate embroidery and is known for its colorful decorative elements and distinctive hat.

Bernese Costume

The traditional dress from Canton Bern includes ornate silver chains and decorative embroidery that varies by region.

Schwingen

Swiss wrestling or "Schwingen" is a traditional sport where wrestlers wear special burlap shorts over their clothes during competitions.

Älplerchilbi

This alpine festival celebrates the return of farmers from summer pastures, featuring traditional costumes and folk music.

Unspunnen Festival

A celebration of Swiss traditions including stone throwing, alphorn blowing, yodeling, and flag throwing in traditional dress.

Basel Fasnacht

Switzerland's largest carnival features elaborate costumes, masks, and parades with participants playing distinctive music.
